TAMAGOTCHI

"Tamagotchi characters are life forms that live on "Tamagotchi Planet", a planet far away from Earth. After their discovery on Earth in 1996, millions of Tamagotchi characters have been raised by people across the world.

Eating great food, and playing many games... The Tamagotchi characters have grown to love the Earthlings who fill them with so much excitement.

Although Tamagotchi characters are a bit selfish at times and sometimes accidentally poop in the room, the days spent with Tamagotchi characters are full of smiles.

Strange and quirky, yet adorable... They will need your care, but will "grow" to be a big part of your everyday life.

That is Tamagotchi."
