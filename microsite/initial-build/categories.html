Original Tamagotchi - Lovely Angel

The Original digital pet you love with a Lovely Angel shell design and three-dimensional wings!
Comes with Angel programming. The device will start with Obaketchi Angel (a ghost character). How you take care of it will decide which of the 5 adult Tamagotchi Angel characters you get.
Feed it, clean up after it, and praise it. You can also play the Shooting Star mini-game!
